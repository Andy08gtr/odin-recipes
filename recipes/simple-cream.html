Simple Cream

Description

This is a simple cream, it isn't supposed to be white like in the image, but who cares. It's delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs (only the red part)
  • 4 spoons sugar
  • 4 spoons of flour
  • the peel of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. mix all ingredients
  2. put in a pan
  3. cook for about 15min
  4. eat
