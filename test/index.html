MY COOKIES

Ingredients: 500g white flour - 200g sugar - 1 sprinkle of salt - 2 eggs - 200g cold butter - 1 packet of yeast - milk

Mix the flour with sugar, salt, eggs, butter into small pieces and for last the sifted yeast. Knead quickly everything, until you obtain a smooth and homogeneous dough. Make some balls as big as as a walnut, flatten them a bit and put them on a baking sheet lined with baking paper. Brush the cookies with milk. Cook in the middle of the preheated oven at 190Â°C for 15-20 minutes.
